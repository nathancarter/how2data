
In mathematics, a series is a sum of values from a sequence, typically
real numbers.  Finite series are written as $a_0+a_1+\cdots+a_n$, or

$$\sum_{i=0}^n a_i.$$

Infinite series are written as $a_0+a_1+a_2+\cdots$, or

$$\sum_{n=0}^\infty a_n.$$
